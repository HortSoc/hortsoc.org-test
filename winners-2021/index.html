SECTION 4

Primary Schools Trophy: Winner — Stoke Poges School

Each year the judging criteria for the Trophy are agreed by representatives of the Society and the schools themselves.

For 2021 each of our 3 Primary Schools in Stoke Poges, Wexham and Fulmer was challenged to grow the heaviest amount of potatoes in a growbag. The theme for the gardens this year was “Rainbow” with the added challenge of creating a scarecrow depicting this subject.
