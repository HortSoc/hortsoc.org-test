## Membership Fees and Benefits

Annual Membership runs from 1st August to 31st July.

- GBP 10 for single membership
- GBP 15 for two persons from the same household
- GBP 20 for family membership (2 adults and children up to age 18, or 25 if in full-time education)
- FREE admission to the Annual Show
- FREE entry in the scheduled classes
- 10% discount at Farnham Common Nurseries and Village Plants, Denham (on production of your membership card)
- A copy of the Annual Show Schedule
- Lecture Evenings, including Spring & Autumn talks with buffet and informal shows
- Three e-newsletters each year (Members only)
- Horticultural advice will be given where and when possible
- Being part of a dynamic and friendly society of like-minded people

Please apply using our online **[Membership Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSclrEro64zUCtoUyt-DCOFbhwarv28c3OaF4L1R0rwSauUL9w/viewform)**.
