<p><strong>SECTION 4</strong></p>
<p><strong>Primary Schools Trophy: Winner — Stoke Poges School</strong></p>
<p>Each year the judging criteria for the Trophy are agreed by representatives of the Society and the schools themselves.</p>
<p>For 2021 each of our 3 Primary Schools in Stoke Poges, Wexham and Fulmer was challenged to grow the heaviest amount of potatoes in a growbag. The theme for the gardens this year was “Rainbow” with the added challenge of creating a scarecrow depicting this subject.</p>
